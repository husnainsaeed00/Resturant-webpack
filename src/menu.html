Chicken Karhai

Chicken Karahi is a beloved dish renowned for its rich and robust flavors. Originating from the heart of South Asian cuisine, this aromatic delight features succulent pieces of chicken cooked with a medley of spices, tomatoes, ginger, and garlic in a traditional Karahi (wok). The dish showcases a perfect balance of tanginess and spiciness, exuding an irresistible aroma that captivates the palate.
